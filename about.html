More Than Magic
It's an Experience

Tom has been fasinated with magic since the age of 5. By eight, he was already producing his own mystery shows - in his mom’s garage.

This passion persisted through his teens and into adulthood. Along the way, he studied under grandmaster magicians such as Jay Scott Berry.

His dedication to the Art has paid off with numerous awards and accolades. The dream he held as a child of becoming a great magician, has now become a reality.

From intimate Close-up to full stage shows, Tom presents a refreshing blend of contemporary and traditional styles, with a touch of originality, all his own.
