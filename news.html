From the Magician's Desk

“There are only two ways to live your life. One is as though nothing is a miracle.
The other is as though everything is a miracle.”
Albert Einstein

The past few years have been very strange for me, other magicians I know, and pretty much everyone else on the planet. Covid brought about challenges and opportunities, but I think the thing that bothered me most was not being with people. I am very social and I relish gathering with new friends, old friends, and family.
As a magician, I feel like the best way to experience magic is live and in person. I can remember the way I felt when I saw my magic teacher vanish a coin or make two sponge balls appear in my hand. It was wonderful!

I just finished the 2022 holiday season, always my busiest time of the year, and I had so much fun performing all over the bay area and beyond. Some of the highlights were performing on a holiday yacht cruise, at a Napa resort wedding, and at a super fun Marin New Year’s Eve party.
2023 is off to a great start, as a colleague and I re-created the magic castle at a private party in San Francisco. We performed five formal close-up shows and performed strolling magic as well. It reminded me of how much I love what I do. I got to attend a ridiculously fun party with people I genuinely enjoyed meeting and spending time with - all as part of my job. The old adage is definitely spot-on - It isn’t work if you love it!
